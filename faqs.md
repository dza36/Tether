## What is Tether?
Tether is a household coordination app that keeps you anchored to the people, rhythms, and responsibilities that matter most. It brings together tasks, events, and occasions — and shares them with the people in your life who need to know.

## How is Tether different from a shared calendar?
A calendar tells you *when* something is happening. Tether tells you *what needs to happen, who's responsible, and whether it got done*. Tasks recur automatically, chores reset, grocery lists sync in real time, and events coordinate attendees and bring lists — all in one place.

## What is a Household?
Your Household is your daily functioning unit — the people you live with. You have one Household, and everything shared within it is automatic. Tasks, events, and grocery lists are all visible to every member.

## What is the difference between a Household and a Group?
Your **Household** is the people you live with — tight, always-on coordination. **Groups** are named collections of people beyond your front door (family, friends, teams). Groups are used for inviting people to events in bulk. Think of them as smart distribution lists.

## How do I invite someone to my Household?
Go to the profile menu → **Household** → enter their email and tap Send. They'll see a banner to accept or decline the next time they open Tether.

## Why can't I see my Household member's tasks?
Tasks are only shared if they are assigned to a Household member or created within a Household context. Personal tasks remain private unless shared.

## What are Occasions?
Occasions are annual, life-significant dates — birthdays, anniversaries, and remembrance days. They surface on your Today tab on the date and in Beyond as they approach. You can create a task or event from any occasion.

## Do I need a Tether account to be remembered on a Remembrance occasion?
No. A name and a date is all you need. Remembrance occasions are intentionally personal — no account link required.

## How does snoozing work?
Swiping left on an item snoozes it. Items snoozed from **Today** move to **This Week**. Items snoozed from **This Week** move to **Beyond**. Nothing disappears — it just moves forward.

## What happens when I complete a recurring task?
Tether automatically calculates the next due date based on your cadence (daily, weekly, monthly, etc.) and advances the task. Your completion history is preserved — nothing is ever deleted.
