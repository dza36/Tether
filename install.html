Install Tether
Stay tethered to what matters
💡 Tether installs directly from your browser — no App Store needed. It works just like a native app.
Steps
1
Open in Chrome
Open the Tether link in Chrome on your Android device. It won't work from Samsung Internet or other browsers.
🌐
2
Tap the three dots menu
Tap the ⋮ menu in the top right corner of Chrome.
⋮
3
Tap "Add to Home screen"
Scroll down in the menu and tap "Add to Home screen." You may also see an install banner appear at the bottom of the screen automatically.
📲
4
Confirm the install
Tap "Add" when prompted. Tether will appear on your home screen like any other app.
✅
5
Open from your home screen
Always open Tether from your home screen icon — not from Chrome. This gives you the full screen experience with no browser bar.
🏠
⚠️ You must use Safari on iPhone or iPad. Installing from Chrome or other browsers on iOS will not work.
Steps
1
Open in Safari
Open the Tether link in Safari. If you're reading this in another browser, copy the link and paste it into Safari.
🧭
2
Tap the Share button
Tap the Share button at the bottom of the screen — it looks like a box with an arrow pointing up ↑
⬆️
3
Tap "Add to Home Screen"
Scroll through the share sheet and tap "Add to Home Screen." It may be a few rows down.
📲
4
Tap "Add"
Confirm the name and tap "Add" in the top right corner.
✅
5
Open from your home screen
Always open Tether from the home screen icon — not from Safari. This gives you the full screen experience with no browser bar.
